HopOff helps you reclaim time from endless scrolling.

Build a library of clips that actually move you — search YouTube or save from TikTok and Instagram with Share.

**Goals that stick**
Set weekly priorities, connect Notion or iOS Shortcuts, and see what you could’ve done with the time you reclaimed.

**Your week at a glance**
Track reclaimed hours, daily averages, and soft spots — the times you’re most likely to drift.

7 days free. Cancel anytime.
