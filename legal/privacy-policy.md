HopOff ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

## What we collect

- **Goals and preferences** you enter in the app (stored on your device).
- **App groups and limits** you configure (stored on your device).
- **Usage statistics** derived from your blocking settings (stored on your device).
- **Motivation videos** you save (metadata and URLs, stored on your device).
- **Subscription status** via RevenueCat / App Store / Google Play (no payment card data on our servers).
- **Optional integrations:** If you connect Notion, we exchange OAuth tokens via our backend to sync goals. We do not sell this data.

## What we send to third parties

| Service | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| OpenRouter (via our API proxy) | Polish goals and generate insights from text you submit |
| YouTube Data API (via our API proxy) | Search for videos you choose to add |
| Notion API (via our API proxy) | OAuth and optional goals sync |
| RevenueCat | Subscription and trial status |

We do not use advertising SDKs.

## Data storage

Most data stays on your device. API requests pass through our serverless proxy so API keys are not embedded in the app. We do not operate a user account database in v1.

## Your choices

- Revoke Notion access in Notion integration settings.
- Cancel subscriptions in App Store / Google Play settings.
- Delete the app to remove on-device data.

## Children

HopOff is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
